2025-03

Version 3.0.0 automates downloading using the Google Photos website instead of the Library API. It is a major change. Google announced in September that the Google Photos Library API would no longer allow "accessing albums and media items not uploaded by your app" after March 31, 2025. A major benefit is the photos downloaded are now the original size. Also, the location data is included if it is shared with you. See how Google Photos protects your location data.
